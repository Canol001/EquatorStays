About Nyawita, Kenya

Nyawita is a tranquil village in Siaya County, located about 20.5 km from Siaya town and 316.1 km from Nairobi. Known for its proximity to Lake Victoria, it offers a peaceful retreat with scenic views and a rich cultural heritage.
